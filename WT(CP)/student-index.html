About TPO

The Training and Placement Officer (TPO) at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT) Pune is responsible for providing career guidance and training assistance to students.
Placement: The TPO helps students achieve their career goals by providing career guidance and training assistance.
Campus placement: Every year, more than 120 companies visit VIT Pune for campus placement.
Student participation: Nearly 1,500 students from different educational backgrounds participate in the placement process.
